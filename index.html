About the Institute

Where Language Meets Excellence

The RV International Language Institute is a specialized language school based in Point Pedro, Sri Lanka, delivering world-class German education entirely online. Your Instructor

Rasika Rasiyasingam

I am an ÖSD and TELC Examiner and experienced German language instructor (A1–C2) with more than three years of professional teaching experience. Born and raised in Switzerland, I am now based in Jaffna, Sri Lanka, where I run the RV International Language Institute — a dedicated online school offering structured, exam-focused German courses.

I prepare learners for major international examinations, including ÖSD, Goethe, telc, fide, and DTZ, and I teach with a clear, efficient, and supportive approach. My lessons combine linguistic accuracy, strong communication training, and modern digital teaching methods to ensure lasting success.

In addition to managing my institute, I also collaborate with several language schools in Germany and Switzerland, teaching online courses and supporting their exam preparation programs. My goal is to provide high-quality, accessible, and professional German education for learners worldwide.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about learning with RV Institute

1

Do I need prior knowledge to join the A1 course?

No, you do not need any prior knowledge. The A1 course is designed for complete beginners. We start with basic vocabulary, pronunciation, and simple grammar step by step.

2

Are the classes online or onsite?

All classes are conducted online via Zoom. You can join from anywhere, and lessons are interactive with speaking practice, grammar training, and exam preparation.

3

How many students are in one class?

We keep our groups small to ensure individual attention. This allows every student to actively participate, especially in speaking practice.

4

Are class recordings provided?

No, class recordings are not provided. All lessons are conducted live and interactive to ensure active participation.

5

Which exam should I choose: Goethe, ÖSD, or telc?

All three exams — Goethe-Institut, ÖSD, and telc GmbH — are internationally recognized. The right choice depends on:

  • Your visa requirements
  • Your destination country
  • The specific institution or embassy requirements
  • Personal preference

We provide structured preparation for all three exams and guide you individually to choose the best option for your goals.
