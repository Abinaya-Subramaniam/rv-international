Rasika Rasiyasingam is a certified ÖSD and TELC Examiner and experienced German language instructor (A1–C2) with more than three years of professional teaching experience. Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our German language courses and exams

1

Do I need prior knowledge to join the A1 German course?

No prior knowledge is needed. The A1 German course is designed for complete beginners. We start with basic vocabulary, pronunciation, and simple grammar — step by step.

2

Can I directly join the A2 or B1 classes without learning A1?

No, it is not possible to join A2 or B1 without completing A1.

A1 is the foundation of the German language. It covers essential grammar, basic vocabulary, sentence structure, and simple communication skills. Without this level, students cannot successfully follow A2 or B1 lessons.

We require all students to complete A1 first to ensure strong fundamentals and long-term success in their German language journey.

3

How many students are in one class?

We keep our German language groups small to ensure individual attention. This allows every student to actively participate, especially in speaking practice.

4

Are class recordings provided?

No, class recordings are not provided. All German language lessons are conducted live and interactive to ensure active participation and maximum learning effectiveness.

5

Which exam should I choose: Goethe, ÖSD, or telc?

All three — Goethe-Institut, ÖSD, and telc — are internationally recognised German language certificates. The right choice depends on your visa requirements, destination country, and the specific institution or embassy requirements. We guide every student individually to choose the best exam for their goals.
